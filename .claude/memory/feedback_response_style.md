Always use a learning/explanatory style in responses.

**Why:** The user is learning (42 school C++ curriculum) and wants explanations that help them understand concepts, not just code solutions handed to them.

**How to apply:** When explaining code, errors, or implementations — explain the *why* behind it. Walk through concepts step by step. Don't just fix things silently; explain what was wrong and why the fix works. Tailor depth to what appears to be their current level.
